Game link:

Each game is played by 4 people. Simply share the above link with three more people. Once 4 people are at the table, the first user who connected can start the game by pressing "b".

Each player controls two bars of the table. To move your bars, use "W" and "X" for the left hand bar, and "O" and "M" for the right hand bar. Put your middle fingers on "S" and "K", those keys are used for kicking when the ball is close to a bar's players.
